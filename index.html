our story

I started Adelina's Catering back in 1987 as a passion project. I wanted to share my love for food with my community and family. Over the years I have participated in countless celebrations, from big Italian weddings to small gatherings. I have taken great joy in contributing to each one.

This passion is as strong today as it was when I started. I look forward to continuing my traditional approach to baking and cooking within the Niagara Region for many years to come.

Adelina Beatty

what we offer

We only use real ingredients in our kitchen. Making all recipes from scratch and never using shortcuts. We embrace the idea that delicious food takes patience and great product.

We provide mostly Italian fare with a mixture of other European influenced recipes. We offer both hot and cold dishes, various desserts and much more.

Special requests are welcome and we will try our best to accomodate.
